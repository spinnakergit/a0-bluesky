# Bluesky Engage Skill

Engage with content and grow presence on Bluesky.

## Triggers
"engage on bluesky", "like posts about", "build bluesky presence"

## Steps
1. Use `bluesky_search` to find relevant content
2. Use `bluesky_read` to review posts
3. Use `bluesky_manage` to like and repost valuable content
4. Use `bluesky_follow` to follow relevant accounts
5. Use `bluesky_post` to reply with thoughtful comments

## Guidelines
- Engage authentically — don't spam likes or follows
- Read posts before engaging to ensure relevance
- Reply with value-adding comments
- Follow accounts that align with user's interests
- Both `uri` and `cid` are needed for likes and reposts
