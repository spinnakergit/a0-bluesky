## bluesky_thread
Post multi-part threads on Bluesky.

**Tier:** All users (free, open protocol)

**Arguments:**
- **posts** (string, required): Thread content, posts separated by `---`
- **separator** (string): Custom separator (default: "---")

~~~json
{"posts": "First post of my thread about AI agents.\n---\nSecond post: here are the key benefits.\n---\nFinal thoughts and conclusion."}
~~~
